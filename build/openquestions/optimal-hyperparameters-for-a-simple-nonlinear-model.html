Open Question 3.1: Optimal hyperparameters for a simple nonlinear model.

In a simple but nontrivial model — say, a linear network of infinite width but finite depth, trained with population gradient descent — what are the optimal choices for the layerwise init scales and learning rates – not just the width scalings but also the constant prefactors? Are they the same or different between layers? Do empirics reveal discernible patterns that theory might aim to explain?
