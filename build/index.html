Understanding deep learning will be the intellectual challenge of the early 21st century, like physics was in the early 20th. A good place to start is the study of neural network training dynamics, since these give rise to everything else. The study of learning mechanics is rapidly developing into a proper subfield of science. This website is meant to serve as a hub, a place to share progress and organize the search for simple, useful understanding.
